Cease the Ships

This project is a mobile board game application, specifically a naval-based board game. It is designed to provide a strategic and engaging experience for players, drawing inspiration from classic board games like chess and The Game of the Generals.

This is one of my proudest projects since I have been working on it with my teammates from the second semester of our third year up until now, in our fourth year. This project has taken me to places I never expected to be part of. Because of this project, my teammates and I won first runner-up at Infotechnolympics 2024. It was an unexpected achievement for me.

Following that event, we were also given the opportunity to represent the University of Makati at the I.T. Skills Olympics 2024 in the Android Application Development (Game Category), where we competed against participants from over 60 schools across the country. Although we didn’t win a place, we are still proud to have represented our campus.
